# Intel RealSense D435i

## Overview

The Intel RealSense D435i is a depth camera that combines stereo vision with integrated inertial measurement units (IMU). It's designed for robotics applications requiring accurate depth perception and motion tracking.

## Specifications

### Depth Sensing
- **Technology**: Stereo vision
- **Depth Accuracy**: ±2% from 0.2m to 10m
- **Depth Range**: 0.2m to 10m (for depth accuracy)
- **Depth Field of View**: 85° × 58° (H × V)

### Color Camera
- **Resolution**: Up to 1920 × 1080
- **Field of View**: 69° × 42° (H × V)
- **Frame Rates**: Up to 30 FPS

### IMU
- **Gyroscope**: ±2000 dps range
- **Accelerometer**: ±16g range
- **IMU Rate**: Up to 400 Hz

### Connectivity
- **Interface**: USB 3.0 Type-C
- **Power**: USB bus-powered or external power
- **Dimensions**: 90mm × 25mm × 25mm
- **Weight**: 55g

## Applications in Robotics

- 3D mapping and reconstruction
- Obstacle detection and avoidance
- Object recognition and pose estimation
- Hand and gesture tracking
- SLAM (Simultaneous Localization and Mapping)

## Advantages

- Integrated IMU for motion tracking
- Good depth accuracy across range
- Robust stereo vision technology
- Excellent ROS integration
- Well-documented SDK

## Considerations

- Performance degrades in bright sunlight
- Requires textured surfaces for stereo matching
- Power consumption via USB can be significant
- May require additional lighting for color camera

## Integration with Robotics Systems

The RealSense D435i integrates well with ROS/ROS2 systems and provides reliable depth information for navigation and manipulation tasks. The integrated IMU helps compensate for motion blur in dynamic applications.

## Getting Started

For robotics applications requiring depth perception, the RealSense D435i provides a reliable solution with good accuracy and integrated motion tracking. The IMU integration makes it particularly suitable for mobile robotics applications.
