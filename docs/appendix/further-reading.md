# Further Reading: Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

## Advanced Textbooks

1. **"Robotics: Modelling, Planning and Control"** by Siciliano, Sciavicco, Villani, and Oriolo
   - Comprehensive coverage of robotics fundamentals with mathematical rigor
   - Essential for understanding advanced kinematic and dynamic modeling

2. **"Introduction to Autonomous Mobile Robots"** by Siegwart, Nourbakhsh, and Scaramuzza
   - Excellent resource for understanding navigation and localization
   - Includes practical implementation examples

3. **"Probabilistic Robotics"** by Thrun, Burgard, and Fox
   - Definitive text on uncertainty in robotics applications
   - Critical for understanding SLAM and sensor fusion

## Specialized Topics

### Embodied Intelligence and Cognition
1. **"The Cambridge Handbook of Embodied Cognition"** by Shapiro
   - Comprehensive overview of embodied cognition research
   - Connects philosophical foundations to practical applications

2. **"Morphological Computation: Nothing but Physical Computation"** by Ziemke and Hesslow
   - Explores how robot morphology contributes to computation
   - Important for understanding efficient robot design

### Deep Learning for Robotics
1. **"Deep Learning"** by Goodfellow, Bengio, and Courville
   - Foundational text for understanding neural networks
   - Essential for implementing learning-based robotic systems

2. **"Reinforcement Learning: An Introduction"** by Sutton and Barto
   - Authoritative text on reinforcement learning
   - Critical for understanding robot learning approaches

## Research Papers and Surveys

### Recent Advances in Physical AI
1. "Physical Reasoning for Interactive and Dexterous Robot Manipulation" - IEEE Transactions on Robotics
   - Latest developments in manipulation research
   - Practical implementations and case studies

2. "A Survey on Deep Learning for Physical Interaction" - Robotics and Autonomous Systems
   - Comprehensive survey of deep learning applications in physical tasks
   - Analysis of current state and future directions

### Humanoid Robotics
1. "Humanoid Robotics: A Reference" by Chen and Asfour
   - Comprehensive reference on humanoid robot design and control
   - Covers both hardware and software aspects

2. "Dynamic Walking and Whole-Body Control for Humanoid Robots" - Advanced Robotics
   - State-of-the-art approaches to humanoid locomotion
   - Implementation techniques and stability analysis
