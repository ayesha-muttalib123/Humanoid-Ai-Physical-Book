# Hardware Requirements

## Overview

This section provides comprehensive information about the hardware platforms, components, and systems used in Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics applications. It covers computing platforms, sensors, actuators, and complete robot systems with current pricing information.

## Hardware Categories

This guide covers the following major hardware categories:

1. **Computing Platforms** - Embedded systems for AI inference and control
2. **Sensors** - Cameras, LiDAR, IMUs, and other perception devices
3. **Robot Platforms** - Complete robot systems for research and development
4. **Audio Systems** - Microphone arrays and audio processing hardware

## Pricing Information

Hardware prices are updated as of 2025 and may vary based on configuration, region, and availability. The prices provided represent typical costs for standard configurations suitable for educational and research purposes.

## Getting Started

For beginners, we recommend starting with the computing platform that matches your project requirements, then adding sensors and actuators as needed. The Jetson Orin Nano Super provides an excellent balance of performance and cost for most applications.
