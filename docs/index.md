# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

**Course Theme - Embodied Intelligence**

Welcome to the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics course! This comprehensive guide explores the fascinating intersection of artificial intelligence and embodied systems, preparing you for the future of robotics and AI integration.

## About This Course

This course covers the fundamental concepts, practical applications, and emerging technologies in physical AI and humanoid robotics. You'll learn about:

- The principles of embodied intelligence
- Hardware and software integration for physical systems
- Real-world applications of humanoid robots
- Laboratory options and practical implementations
