# 11.2 Humanoid Locomotion

## Introduction

Humanoid locomotion represents one of the most challenging aspects of humanoid robotics, requiring sophisticated control algorithms to achieve stable and efficient walking patterns. This chapter explores the principles and control of humanoid robot locomotion.
